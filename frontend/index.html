Revolutionizing Rural Education

Manual attendance systems in rural schools have been a major challenge, leading to inefficiencies and errors. Our solution brings automation and accuracy to the forefront.

  • Save time and improve efficiency
  • Accurate attendance records
  • Low-cost and easy to deploy

Problem Description

Many rural schools in India rely on manual attendance systems, which are time-consuming and prone to errors. Teachers spend significant time marking attendance, reducing instructional time. Additionally, inaccurate records can lead to discrepancies in government reporting for schemes like mid-day meals.

Impact

This issue affects over 50% of rural schools, impacting millions of students and teachers. It leads to inefficiencies, delays in reporting, and potential mismanagement of resources.

Expected Outcomes

A low-cost, user-friendly software or mobile application that automates attendance using facial recognition or RFID-based systems. Requires minimal infrastructure and training for deployment in rural schools.

Stakeholders

School administrators, teachers, students, and government education departments will benefit from this solution.

Key Features

Designed specifically for rural schools with limited infrastructure

Time Saving

Reduce attendance marking time from 10 minutes to just 2 minutes. More time for teaching and learning.

99% Accuracy

Eliminate manual errors and ensure precise attendance records for government reporting and meal schemes.

Cost Effective

Secure & Reliable
